boy - A childish Man replacement

If you've ever written a man file, you know the struggle. Arcane syntax ahoy! Boy is designed to clean the mess up with a simple, fuzzy-evaluated "boy file". Boy provides the utilities for reading information quicker than man with headers. Rather than scrolling through an entire curses display, just pick the relevant goodies that you want to read right away.
